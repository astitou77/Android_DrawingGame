*To open the original project 'DrawingApp.aia' in App Inventor:

1- Login to your App Inventor 2 account
2- Go to Projects > Import project (.aia) from my computer...
3- select 'DrawingApp.aia' file

*To install the apk Build 'DrawingApp.apk' in Android devices:

1- Send the file 'DrawingApp.apk' to your E-mail
2- open 'DrawingApp.apk' in your Android device's E-mail app
3- open the installed App 'DrawingApp'
